Frequently Asked Questions

What are AI Agent Skills?

Skills are instruction files (markdown) that enhance what your AI agent can do. Think of them as "plugins" or "mods" for AI.

How do I install a skill?

After purchase, copy the skill content and paste it into your AI's system prompt or rules file. See our How It Works section for detailed guides.

Which AI agents are supported?

Skills work with Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Windsurf, and any AI that accepts system prompts or custom instructions.

Can I share skills with my team?

Individual purchases are for personal use. For team or enterprise use, contact us for licensing options.

How do I become a developer?

Sign up, then apply through your dashboard. Once approved, you can upload and sell your own skills.
